About Us

Our mission is to protect job seekers from scams by providing a trusted platform to verify job and internship listings.

🎯 Our Mission

Scam Job & Internship Detector is built to empower individuals in identifying and reporting fake job offers. We aim to create a safer digital hiring environment for everyone.

👥 Who We Are

We are a team of developers, cybersecurity enthusiasts, and job safety advocates working to provide a free, easy-to-use, and intelligent solution to job scams.

🔒 Why It Matters

Every day, thousands fall victim to job scams. Our platform helps identify red flags, educates users, and allows the community to report fraudulent listings.
